Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how 4UrClass uses cookies on our website, 4urclass.app. By using our Service, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It helps the website remember actions and preferences between pages.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies only for the core functionality of the website. The primary cookie in use is a first-party session cookie used for authentication and session management.

  • Authentication and session management: keeps you signed in while you move through the site.
  • Security: the session cookie is cryptographically signed to reduce tampering risk.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly necessary cookies: these are essential for the website to function correctly. Without them, login and account access would not work.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Most browsers allow some control over cookies. If you block our essential session cookie, the website will not function properly and you will not be able to use account features as intended.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this policy when our operational, legal, or regulatory requirements change. Please revisit this page periodically for the latest version.
