What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It enables the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the sole purpose of ensuring the core functionality of our website. The cookie we use is a first-party session cookie, which is essential for the operation of our Service.

  • Authentication and Session Management: When you log into your account, we use a session cookie to keep you logged in as you navigate through different pages of our website. This cookie allows us to recognize you as the same user and ensures that you do not have to log in repeatedly during a single browsing session.
  • Security: Our session cookie is cryptographically signed to prevent tampering and enhance the security of your account.
